Privacy Policy for Psychro Weather - 
Effective Date: May 14, 2026



1. Introduction
Psychro Weather ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.


2. Information Collection and Use
Location Data
  To provide accurate local weather forecasts, Wet Bulb Globe Temperature (WBGT) safety metrics, Wind Chill Index (WCI) safety metrics and emergency storm alerts (Sachet/NDMA alerts), the App requires access to your location.

  Precise Location: We collect precise location data (GPS and network-based) to provide weather information for your specific coordinates.

  Background Location: Because the App provides time-sensitive storm alerts and persistent status bar updates, we collect and process your location data even when the App is closed or not in use. This is necessary to ensure you receive life-saving weather warnings for your current area automatically.

Device Information
  We may collect basic device information (such as model, OS version, and connectivity status) to ensure the App functions correctly and to help us troubleshoot technical issues.


3. Third-Party Services
  We use trusted third-party providers to deliver our services. These providers have their own privacy policies:

  Open-Meteo: Used for fetching weather data and 15-minute precipitation forecasts.

  NDMA (Sachet): Used to fetch official government weather alerts via RSS feeds.

  Postal PIN Code API: We may send your PIN code to this service to resolve your local district name for more accurate alert matching.

  Google Firebase: We use Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to understand app usage and improve stability. No personally identifiable information is sent to Firebase.


4. Data Storage and Security
  Local Storage: Your coordinates and last-fetched weather data are stored locally on your device (using SharedPreferences) to allow the App to function offline.

Encryption: All data transmitted between the App and our API providers is encrypted via HTTPS.

  No Selling of Data: We do not sell, trade, or rent your location or usage data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.


5. Permissions
  You will be asked to grant the following permissions:

  Location (Foreground & Background): Mandatory for weather and safety alerts.

  Notifications: Required to display the persistent weather status bar and emergency alerts.

  Internet: Required to fetch current weather data.


6. Children's Privacy
  Psychro Weather does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. The App is a general weather utility designed for all users.


7. Changes to This Policy
  We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date.
